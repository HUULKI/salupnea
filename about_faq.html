HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER ON THE SITE?

Select the required item and click on the "Buy" button. When you decide on your selection, click on "Proceed to checkout" and follow the instructions. On the page that opens, fill in all the required fields, select the shipping method and enter, if necessary, its address. You can enter a discount promo code in the appropriate field. The cost of the order will be recalculated automatically. Then click on the "Pay" button and enter your payment details. After payment you will return to the main page of the site, and you will receive an email about the payment for the order and a cash receipt. By clicking on the "Pay" and proceeding to the stage of payment you agree to the processing of your personal data. Please read the document "Regulations on the storage and protection of personal data of julieshop18.com service users" in order to avoid conflicts! Delivery data will be sent to your e-mail address.

I DID EVERYTHING ACCORDING TO THE INSTRUCTIONS, BUT PAYMENT FOR THE ORDER DID NOT GO THROUGH. WHY?

Try to choose another payment method.

I WANT TO CANCEL/CHANGE MY ORDER (COMPLETELY OR PARTIALLY).

Make sure that your order is correct at the time of placing it. If you decide to cancel your order partially or completely, let us know by emailing julieshop18@gmail.com In the subject line, specify your order number and brief information, and in the email, please, describe what you want to change. We will definitely contact you and give you the necessary instructions. If you cancel an order that has already been shipped, no shipping charges will be refunded.

I WANT TO ADD MORE ITEMS TO MY ORDER.

Make sure that your order has not been sent yet (you can check in the letters from us to the specified email and in your personal account). After that, you must notify us by email julieshop18@gmail.com (specify the numbers of all orders you want to bundle into one parcel)

I WANT TO RESERVE THE ITEM(S) BEFORE PAYMENT.

The product is assigned to the buyer only after the payment for the order.
